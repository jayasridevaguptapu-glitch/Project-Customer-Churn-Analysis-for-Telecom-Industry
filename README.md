# Customer Churn Analysis for Telecom Industry

## Project Overview

Customer churn is a major challenge in the telecom industry. This project aims to analyze customer behavior and predict whether a customer is likely to leave the telecom service. Machine Learning techniques were used to identify churn patterns and generate business recommendations for improving customer retention.

## Objective

* Predict customer churn using Machine Learning.
* Identify key factors influencing customer attrition.
* Segment customers into Loyal, At Risk, and Dormant groups.
* Provide actionable business recommendations.

## Dataset

Dataset: Telco Customer Churn Dataset

Features:

* Gender
* Senior Citizen
* Tenure
* Monthly Charges
* Total Charges
* Contract Type
* Internet Service
* Payment Method
* Churn

## Customer Segmentation

### Loyal Customers

Customers with low churn probability.

### At Risk Customers

Customers with moderate churn probability.

### Dormant Customers

Customers with high churn probability and likely to leave.

## Key Findings

* Month-to-month contract customers show higher churn.
* Customers with high monthly charges are more likely to churn.
* Short-tenure customers have higher attrition rates.
* Long-term customers are more loyal.

## Business Recommendations

* Promote long-term contracts.
* Offer loyalty rewards.
* Improve customer support services.
* Provide personalized retention offers.
* Engage high-risk customers proactively.
